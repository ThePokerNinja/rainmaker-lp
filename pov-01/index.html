A new agentic platform, built by Santa Cruz Studios

System Design by Michael Stewman

Santa Cruz, CA · June 2026 Pre-release · Methodology reviewed

Santa Cruz Studios is preparing a unified agent platform — voice-first, tool-connected, and designed for operators who need one reliable surface instead of a stack of tabs. This update shares direction and measured targets, not product internals.

The platform centers on Samuel, the Systems Agent Model (or S.A.M. for short) — a single customer-facing agent that coordinates specialized capabilities — scheduling, design, sales, and trading research — through a shared orchestration layer. Early benchmarks focus on conversational latency, unit economics, and deployment cost — not marketing claims.1

Private pilots are underway. A broader early-access cohort will open after core instrumentation and consent infrastructure are verified in production.
